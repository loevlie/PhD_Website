A digital garden

Notes from a PhD in machine learning.

This is less a blog than a working notebook. I write here when something I've been thinking about reaches the point of "I should explain this to myself" — usually about ML research, sometimes about life in Amsterdam, occasionally about whatever else won't leave me alone.

Each note has a maturity tag — 🌱seedling for half-formed ideas, 🌿budding for ideas being developed, and 🌳evergreen for things I'm comfortable standing behind. They get tended over time.
